SO WHAT IS PROYECTO?

PROYECTO is a providing /hiring service app where in individuals who want to get their educational work done ,for instance writing records, making power point presentations, excel sheets, posters, logos, models etc. can reach out to people who are exceptional in the specific field . Its basically a middleman app that provides mainly educational based services

Our app also helps the one delivering these services generate money and utilize their skills in a much efficient way, honing them. It brings different domains under one umbrella where people could request as well as provide services to each other at the same time.
